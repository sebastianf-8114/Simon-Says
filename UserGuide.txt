How to Play Simon Says:


Once Ran:

The Canvas will load up and flash a random sequence. 

This will flash for 0.6 seconds. Please click on the correct order of the lights that are flashed.

Note*: Please wait an 0.6 after each button flashes before pressing on a button. If you do not wait, the game will end.

If you click on the wrong button, or if you ddo not wait 0.6 seconds after it darkens, the game will end.

If you match the sequence of lights, another light will be added to the sequence, and the new sequence will be displayed.
